Co-Curricular Activities

Discover, develop, and excel in your unique talents

Beyond the Classroom

At Nyanchwa Adventist Preparatory School, we believe in developing the whole child. Our comprehensive co-curricular program provides opportunities for students to discover their passions, build confidence, develop teamwork skills, and excel in areas beyond academics.

Every student participates in at least one co-curricular activity, ensuring balanced development and exposure to diverse experiences.

Sports & Athletics

Building healthy bodies and competitive spirits

Football

Both boys' and girls' teams compete in inter-school tournaments. Professional coaching develops technical skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Boys & Girls Teams

Athletics

Track and field events including sprints, long-distance running, high jump, long jump, and relay races. Regular training and competition opportunities.

All Ages

Netball & Basketball

Team sports that build coordination, strategic thinking, and cooperation. Inter-class and inter-school competitions held regularly.

Mixed Teams

Table Tennis

Improves hand-eye coordination, reflexes, and concentration. Indoor sport suitable for all weather conditions and skill levels.

Individual Sport

Volleyball

Team sport promoting agility, teamwork, and quick decision-making. Competitive teams participate in zonal tournaments.

Team Sport

Swimming

Swimming lessons and competitive training. Life-saving skill combined with excellent physical fitness and confidence building.

Seasonal Program

Clubs & Societies

Explore interests and develop new skills

Music & Choir

Vocal training, instrumental music, and choir performances at school events and competitions.

Drama & Theatre

Acting, stagecraft, and performance skills through plays, skits, and dramatic presentations.

Debate Club

Public speaking, critical thinking, and persuasive communication through structured debates.

Science Club

Hands-on experiments, science fairs, and exploration of scientific concepts beyond the curriculum.

Reading Club

Promoting literacy, book discussions, storytelling, and love for reading across all genres.

ICT & Coding Club

Computer skills, basic programming, digital literacy, and technology exploration.

Environment Club

Tree planting, recycling projects, environmental conservation, and sustainability awareness.

Art & Craft

Painting, drawing, sculpture, and creative crafts that develop artistic expression and fine motor skills.

Creative Writing

Poetry, stories, essays, and journalism. Students publish work in the school magazine.

Math Club

Challenging problems, math competitions, puzzles, and games that make mathematics fun.

Community Service

Outreach programs, charity work, and community development projects teaching social responsibility.

Leadership Club

Developing future leaders through mentorship, decision-making skills, and responsibility training.
