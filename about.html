About Our School

Discover our story, values, and commitment to excellence

Our Mission

To provide quality Christ-centered education that nurtures the intellectual, spiritual, physical, and social development of every child, preparing them to be responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be a leading Christian preparatory school in the region, recognized for academic excellence, strong moral values, and holistic child development that transforms lives and communities.

Our Story

Nyanchwa Adventist Preparatory School was established with a vision to provide quality Christian education to the children of Kisii Central and beyond. Located in Nyaribari Chache Constituency, our school has grown to become a trusted institution for holistic child development.

Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence while staying true to our Adventist values. Our experienced and dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to ensure that every child receives personalized attention and the best possible foundation for their future.

We take pride in our modern facilities, comprehensive curriculum, and the positive impact we continue to make in the lives of our students and the broader community.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Faith

We integrate Christian values into every aspect of learning, building strong spiritual foundations.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, character, and service to our community.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our dealings and relationships.

Compassion

We foster a caring environment where every child feels valued, loved, and supported.

Community

We build strong partnerships with parents and the community for collective growth.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods and technology to enhance learning experiences.

Discipline

We maintain a structured environment that promotes responsibility and self-control.

Growth

We encourage continuous personal, academic, and spiritual development for all learners.

Message from the Headteacher

"Welcome to Nyanchwa Adventist Preparatory School!"

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to our school community. At Nyanchwa, we believe that every child is unique and possesses unlimited potential waiting to be unlocked through quality education and nurturing guidance.

Our dedicated team of professional educators is committed to providing a safe, supportive, and stimulating learning environment where children can thrive academically, socially, spiritually, and physically. We combine rigorous academic standards with strong Christian values to develop well-rounded individuals who will make positive contributions to society.

We recognize that parents are our most important partners in education. Together, we work to ensure that each child receives the personalized attention they need to reach their full potential. Our doors are always open, and we encourage active parent involvement in our school activities.

I invite you to explore our programs, visit our facilities, and become part of the Nyanchwa family. Together, we can shape bright futures for our children.

God bless you,
