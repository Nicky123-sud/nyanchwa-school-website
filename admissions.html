Admission Requirements

What you need to enroll your child

Pre-Primary (PP1 & PP2)

Age Requirements

  • PP1: Child must be at least 3 years old by January of admission year
  • PP2: Child must be at least 4 years old by January of admission year
Required Documents:
  • Copy of child's birth certificate
  • Immunization card
  • 2 passport-size photos
  • Parent/guardian ID copies
  • Completed application form
Primary (Grade 1-6)

Age Requirements

  • Grade 1: Child must be at least 6 years old
  • Grades 2-6: Transfer students with appropriate grade level
Required Documents:
  • Copy of child's birth certificate
  • Transfer certificate from previous school
  • Most recent progress report
  • 2 passport-size photos
  • Parent/guardian ID copies
  • Completed application form

Application Process

Simple steps to enroll your child

1
Visit/Inquiry

Visit our school or contact us for more information about our programs and admission requirements.

2
Application Form

Obtain and complete the admission application form. Available at the school office or downloadable online.

3
Submit Documents

Submit completed application form with all required documents to the admissions office.

4
Assessment & Interview

Child assessment (for Grade 1+) and parent interview with the admissions team.

5
Admission Decision

Receive admission decision within 3-5 working days via phone or email notification.

6
Fee Payment

Pay registration fee and first term fees to secure your child's place in the school.

7
Orientation

Attend parent and student orientation to familiarize with school policies and procedures.

8
Welcome!

Your child begins their exciting educational journey at Nyanchwa Adventist School!
