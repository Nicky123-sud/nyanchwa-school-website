Welcome to Nyanchwa Adventist Preparatory School

Located in the vibrant community of Kisii Central, Nyaribari Chache Constituency, Nyanchwa Adventist Preparatory School stands as a beacon of quality Christian education in the region.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to providing a nurturing environment where children develop academically, spiritually, socially, and physically. Our dedicated team of qualified educators ensures that every child receives personalized attention and the tools they need to succeed.

We believe in holistic education that combines academic excellence with strong Christian values, preparing our students not just for exams, but for life.

Our Programs

Quality education from early years through primary school

Pre-Primary

PP1 & PP2

Foundation years focusing on play-based learning, early literacy, numeracy, and social skills development in a safe and caring environment.

  • Play-based learning
  • Early literacy & numeracy
  • Social skills development
  • Creative activities
Lower Primary

Grade 1-3

Building strong foundations in core subjects while developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills through engaging activities.

  • Core subject mastery
  • Reading proficiency
  • Mathematical reasoning
  • Environmental awareness
Upper Primary

Grade 4-6

Advanced learning preparing students for secondary education with emphasis on academic excellence, leadership, and character development.

  • CBC curriculum mastery
  • Leadership development
  • Project-based learning
  • Exam preparation
